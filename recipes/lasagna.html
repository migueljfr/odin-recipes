Lasagna

Description

This Lasagna recipe will absolutely blow your socks off! There is so much flavor in the cheese, meat, and sauce that it will revolutionize the way that you look at lasagna.

The ingredients and steps below will allow you to follow along on a journey that can change lives, but more importantly fill stomachs.

Ingredients

Cheese Filling

  • 15 oz. ricotta cheese
  • 1 Large Egg
  • 2 cups mozzarella cheese
  • 3/4 cup Parmesan Cheese
  • 2 teaspoons Italian seasoning
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 tsp pepper

Meat Sauce

  • 1 TSBP olive oil
  • 1 yellow onion, finely diced
  • 3/4 lb. ground beef
  • 3/4 lb. ground Italian sausage
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
